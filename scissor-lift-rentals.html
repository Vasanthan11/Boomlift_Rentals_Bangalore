Safety features on our scissor lifts

Our scissor lifts include essential safety interlocks and features such as emergency stop buttons, guard-railed platforms, tilt and overload alarms, and descent controls from both platform and ground.

We recommend using full-body harnesses where required, marking safe working zones on the floor, and keeping the platform free from unnecessary material to maintain safe working loads.
