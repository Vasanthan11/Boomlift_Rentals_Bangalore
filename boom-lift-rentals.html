Telescopic boom lifts

Articulating boom lifts

Approx. 40 ft – 80 ft

Multiple joints allow the boom to move up and over obstacles, making it perfect for working around machines, mezzanines, pipe racks and building projections inside or outside the plant.

Common uses: plant maintenance, atriums, congested industrial areas.

Why boom lifts

Why rent a boom lift instead of scaffolding or ladders?

Renting a boom lift gives you faster setup and safer access, especially on projects that move quickly or demand frequent repositioning of the working height.

  • Reduced setup time – drive to the spot, elevate and start work in minutes.
  • Improved safety – stable platform with guardrails and controls at the basket.
  • Reach over obstacles – go up and over machinery, parked vehicles and structures.
  • Flexible working heights – adjust height and outreach as the job moves.
  • Lower labour dependency – fewer people required compared to scaffolding erection.

For time-bound or shutdown projects in Bangalore, boom lifts can significantly cut access time and keep more focus on actual work instead of temporary access systems.
